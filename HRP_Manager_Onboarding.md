# HRP Manager Onboarding & Learning Plan

To get started, we need to establish your stable's current state and your managing preferences.

## 1. Plan to Learn HRP Fast
I have already reviewed `docs/SETUP.md` and `docs/MECHANICS.md`. I see there is an existing automated pipeline to scrape horse data, and existing mechanic documentation. My immediate learning plan:

1. **Read Existing Mechanics Docs**: I will review the structured topic files in `docs/mechanics/` to learn the training, entry rules, breeding, and stamina systems.
2. **Review Forum Intel**: I will read `docs/FORUM_INTEL.md` for competitive strategies and "meta" gameplay tactics.
3. **Analyze Initial Export**: Once we have your account data in `HRP_Tracker.xlsx`, I will analyze your current roster to see real data on stamina, condition, forms, and results to cement my understanding.

**Data Ingestion:**
Since we have an automation pipeline out of the box (`SETUP.md`), please do the following to get me your horse roster data securely:
1. Open up your Windows PowerShell in the `c:\hrp-ops` directory.
2. Run `python scripts\01_login_save_state.py` (or `.\.venv\Scripts\python scripts\01_login_save_state.py` if using the venv). 
3. A browser will appear. Please log in manually. The script will save your session state to `inputs\export\auth.json` and close.
4. Let me know when you've done this, and I can trigger `scripts\RUN_FULL_REBUILD.bat` to ingest your stable roster.

*(Alternatively, if you prefer not to use the script, please export your horse roster into `inputs\export\` or provide a CSV/Excel file of your current horses, their ages, stamina/condition, etc.)*

## 3. Stable Manager System (v1)
I have created the following files to serve as our management system backbone:
- **`HRP_Playbook.md`**: Our living knowledge base for rules, strategies, and empirical discoveries.
- **`Stable_Dashboard.md`**: A quick-glance view of active horses, upcoming obligations, and bankroll (fed by our Tracker).
- **`Weekly_Plan.md`**: Outlines scheduled works, race targets, and breeding plans for the next 7 days.
- **`Decisions_Log.md`**: A history of all major strategic decisions, their EV rationale, required approvals, and eventual outcomes.
- **`HRP_Tracker.xlsx`**: The master dataset (auto-generated by the scripts).

Once you provide the requested data and run the login script, I will run the "Daily Stable Manager Checklist" for the first time!
