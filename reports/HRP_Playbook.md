# HRP Playbook — Living Knowledge Base
**Last Updated:** 2026-03-17

## Critical Rules
1. **Condition < 50 = PERMANENT degradation.** Keep CND at 95+ always.
2. **Auto-scratch at 75.** CND AND STA must be >= 75 at race cutoff. Target >= 80.
3. **90-day work rule.** Must have qualifying timed work at track within 90 days.
4. **Consistency sweet spot: 2-4 activities/period.** 0-1 = drops. 6+ = drops.
5. **Training mode exit penalty: 3 periods.** Switch to racing mode 3+ periods before race.
6. **No 'Light Train' option.** Only: Standard, Std-Short/Long, Heavy, Hvy-Short/Long.
7. **Racing drains 53-69% stamina.** Plan 5-10 days recovery post-race.
8. **Nightly maintenance:** CND drops 1-10 (yearlings) to 1-4 (4+). STA recovers 6-14.

## Power Rating (Dual-Track)
- **Raced:** SRF Best 60% + Consistency 20% + ITM 10% + Works 10%
- **Virgin:** Works Quality 70% + Consistency 30%
- **Excluded:** Fitness (CND/STA) and Win Rate

## Proven Strategies
- **3:1 Breeze-to-Handily ratio** — breezing builds sustainably, handily risks fatigue
- **Double-dip:** SB wins don't burn open allowance conditions
- **Small fields (4-7)** dramatically increase win probability
- **Gelding** can unlock hidden speed in colts with plateauing SRF
- **Post-race:** 5 days rest + 2 breezes over 10 days = 90% ready
