# Training Mode vs Racing Mode

## Overview
When placing an active horse at a farm, you can select Racing Mode or Training Mode. Training Mode is only available at farms, not at tracks.

---

## Condition and Stamina in Training Mode

Topic: Training Mode Meter Convergence
Claim: In Training Mode, both condition and stamina meters move toward 100 during nightly maintenance and stop at 100 once reached. This is different from Racing Mode where condition goes down and stamina goes up during maintenance.
Source URL: https://www.horseracingpark.com/help/index.aspx (Section 15)
Snippet: "If a horse is placed in training mode its condition and stamina meters will each move towards 100 during maintenance and stop at 100 once it is reached."
Confidence: High

---

## Consistency Protection in Training Mode

Topic: Training Mode Consistency Protection
Claim: In Training Mode, the consistency meter does NOT move down during maintenance regardless of works+races in the last 30 days. This is unique to training mode — normally, 0, 1, or 6+ works+races would cause consistency to drop.
Source URL: https://www.horseracingpark.com/help/index.aspx (Section 15)
Snippet: "The consistency meter will also not move down during maintenance no matter the number of works plus races in the last 30 days if the horse is in training mode."
Confidence: High

---

## Consistency Penalty on Exit (Exact Details)

Topic: Training Mode Consistency Penalty
Claim: The "100% consistency penalty" does NOT mean consistency drops to 0 permanently. It means a penalty is APPLIED that treats the consistency meter as if it were 0 for race purposes. The penalty decays over 3 maintenance periods:
- 0 maintenance periods passed: 100% penalty → consistency treated as 0
- 1 maintenance period passed: 66% penalty
- 2 maintenance periods passed: 33% penalty
- 3 maintenance periods passed: penalty gone completely
The horse must be changed to Racing Mode or relocated to a track before a race, and needs 3 maintenance periods for the penalty to fully wear off.
Source URL: https://www.horseracingpark.com/help/index.aspx (Section 15)
Snippet: "It will take 3 maintenance periods for the consistency penalty to wear off completely. Each maintenance period that passes will reduce the amount of penalty. If no maintenance periods have passed and the horse races it will incur a 100% penalty so that its consistency meter will be 0. If one maintenance period has passed it will incur a 66% penalty. If two maintenance periods have passed it will incur a 33% penalty."
Confidence: High

## Penalty Display

Topic: Training Mode Penalty Indicator
Claim: If a horse is going to incur a consistency penalty, it will be displayed beside the consistency meter. After the brackets you may see "[N,M]" where N is maintenance periods remaining to remove penalty and M is what consistency would be if raced now.
Source URL: https://www.horseracingpark.com/help/index.aspx (Sections 15, 19)
Snippet: "If a horse is going to incur a penalty it will be displayed beside the consistency meter."
Confidence: High

---

## Can Timed Works Be Performed in Training Mode?

Topic: Timed Works in Training Mode
Claim: Yes, timed works can be performed while in Training Mode. The help page states that training mode affects only how meters move DURING MAINTENANCE. Works and training can still be given normally. The consistency meter notes about "works+races in the last 30 days" specifically mention that these rules are suspended in training mode for maintenance purposes, but the works themselves still occur and affect meters in the normal way when performed.
Source URL: https://www.horseracingpark.com/help/index.aspx (Sections 15, 22)
Confidence: High

---

## Location Restriction

Topic: Training Mode Only at Farms
Claim: Training Mode is only available at farms, not at tracks. When placing an active horse at a farm, you have 2 modes: Racing or Training. Tracks do not offer this option — horses at tracks are always in racing mode.
Source URL: https://www.horseracingpark.com/help/index.aspx (Section 15)
Snippet: "When placing an active horse at the farm you will have 2 modes you can place it in. You can place it in racing or training mode."
Confidence: High
