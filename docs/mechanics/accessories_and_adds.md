# Accessories and Adds

## Overview
The confirmed in-game accessories/medications are: **Blinkers, Bute, Lasix, Shadow Roll, and Gelding**. There is NO "Front Wraps" option despite an outdated reference in the verify benefits help page.

---

## Blinkers

Topic: Blinkers Mechanical Effect
Claim: Blinkers narrow a horse's line of sight. Some horses will perform better with blinkers, others will not like them. The effect is horse-specific — there is no universal benefit.
Source URL: https://www.horseracingpark.com/help/index.aspx (Section 36)
Snippet: "Blinkers are used to narrow a horse's line of sight. Some will perform better with blinkers and others will not like them."
Confidence: High

## Shadow Roll

Topic: Shadow Roll Mechanical Effect

## Lasix

Topic: Lasix Mechanical Effect
Claim: Lasix is a medication used mainly to help bleeders. Some horses perform better on Lasix, others will not. Two additional effects are noted: (1) a horse may be MORE CONSISTENT when on Lasix, and (2) it may have "more desire to win races in the stretch." This suggests Lasix affects both the consistency dimension and the stretch-run desire factor.
Source URL: https://www.horseracingpark.com/help/index.aspx (Section 36)
Snippet: "Lasix is a medication used mainly to help bleeders. Some horses will perform better when placed on it and others will not. You may also notice a horse is more consistent when on lasix and/or it has more desire to win races in the stretch."
Confidence: High

## Bute

Topic: Bute Mechanical Effect
Claim: Bute is a medication used to help aching joints. Some horses perform better on Bute, others will not. An additional effect: Bute "may slow the aging process slightly" for horses that respond to it. This suggests Bute can extend a horse's competitive lifespan.
Source URL: https://www.horseracingpark.com/help/index.aspx (Section 36)
Snippet: "Bute is a medication used to help aching joints. Some horses will perform better when placed on it and others will not. You may also notice bute may slow the aging process slightly if it is a horse that runs better with bute."
Confidence: High

## Gelding

Topic: Gelding Mechanical Effect
Claim: Yes, gelding has mechanical effects beyond removing breeding eligibility. Male horses can be gelded to "help their speed and consistency." The impact diminishes with age — the older a horse gets, the less likely a geld will have an impact. Gelding permanently removes the ability to breed. A gelded retired horse is automatically deactivated since it cannot breed.
Source URL: https://www.horseracingpark.com/help/index.aspx (Section 36)
Snippet: "Male horses can be gelded to try and help their speed and consistency. The older a horse gets the less likely a geld will have an impact on them. A gelded male can not breed."
Confidence: High

## Timing Restrictions

Topic: Accessories/Medications Near Races
Claim: You cannot alter instructions, accessories, or medications after the scratch deadline (1 hour before post time, unless race description says otherwise). Once medications are added, they cannot be removed until the next maintenance period.
Source URL: https://www.horseracingpark.com/help/index.aspx (Section 28)
Snippet: "You will not be able to alter instructions/accessories/medications or train a horse after the scratch deadline period. Please note once medications are added, they can not be removed until the next maintenance period."
Confidence: High
